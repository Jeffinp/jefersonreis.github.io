About Me

My name is Jeferson Reis, I'm 18 years old, and since I was 3 years old when I first encountered a computer, I fell in love with computing. I've always been a fan of computers. Before becoming a programmer, I was an IT technician, optimizing Windows for various people who needed a faster system. Then, I started repairing the physical parts of computers, as well as working with printers, consoles, and even cell phones. However, what I really wanted was programming. I tried to develop games in C#, but at the time, I wasn't successful due to lack of knowledge. Nowadays, I've studied and become a Full-Stack developer, always seeking to learn more, working hard, studying, and improving my skills in what I love to do.

I've also worked as a graphic designer, creating banners and logos for print shops, companies, and freelancers. Additionally, I was a digital artist, making illustrations for various people and companies that requested digital art from me.
